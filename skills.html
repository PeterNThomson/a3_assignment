Skills & Jobs

As the manager of a team of 4 to deliver the project outcome, I am responsible for planning and overseeing the project within a reasonable timeframe, from the initial create of the idea, to the completion of the project. Efficient time management skills to ensure the meeting of set due dates is required, as well as coordinating tasks to my team of 4, ensuring projects are delivered on time, within the budget and the desired outcome aligns to the objectives. For the team of 4, the following roles are to be fulfilled: Software Engineer, Programmer, Graphic Designer and Software Developer. This combined team can work together in either an office setting or remotely from home, remembering collaboration and communication is key when working on this project.

Lead Software Engineer:

The Lead Software Engineer is responsible for applying the principles of engineering to the development, maintenance, testing and software evaluation of the overlay. Experience in leading a small team to design, build and maintain the software is crucial. As the lead software role for this creation, this role is the key component for commencement in the project.

Computer Programmer:

Extensive knowledge of the Python language, as well as experience in a similar role using Python is needed for this job role. Not only will the selected employee be writing the code for the overlay, but also completing the tasks of testing the program, correcting coding errors, and reviewing and rewriting the program when needed. The successful candidate must have a proven record of submitting projects at the required deadline and alongside software developers and software engineers.

Graphic Designer:

A Graphic Designer is an essential member of this team, in creating the aesthetic design of the overlay. Experience in creating similar designs for programs is necessary, along with innovative thinking, and working alongside a Project Manager is required to create the perfect look for the overlay.

Software Developer:

A Software Developer is needed to not only problem solve and diagnose, but also create efficient algorithms, integrate the Tekore Library into the overlay, and identify areas of improvement based on not only testing, but feedback from Beta users. Experience in innovative thinking, as well as working as a team alongside Software Engineers and Computer Programmers is essential in this job role, as well as having the ability to accept positive and negative feedback and move forward with necessary changes based on this feedback when needed.

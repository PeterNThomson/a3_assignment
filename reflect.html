Group Reflection

Peter

This assignment period saw all group members having to deal with various life challenges thrown in the way, despite this fact we persevered and were able to produce an assignment that we were happy with, given the time constraints we encountered. Because of the we were unable to schedule regular meetings and as a result communication was lacking, we ended up defaulting back to Discord as a method of fast communication between group members because it is an application we are all familiar with, this was able to kickstart communication and get us back on the right track. In regard to GitHub commit trails for the website, as with last assignment it doesn't accurately display individual group members efforts towards our submission. We decided to delegate tasks out to align with everyone's strengths, while this approach diminished the ability for everyone to try their hand at something we didn't have a lot of experience in. But when life give you lemons, you make lemonade.
